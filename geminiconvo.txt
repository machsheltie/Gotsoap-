# Project Specification: "Got Soap" Web Campaign
## Multi-Page Immersive Viewport & Interactive PSA Engine

### 1. Conceptual Intent & Art Direction
*   **The Mission:** A premium, high-fashion digital satire of the iconic 1990s "Got Milk?" print campaigns, targeting low-effort dating app culture[cite: 1].
*   **The Strategy:** The application uses a multi-page routing system where individual pages utilize deconstructed poster assets as full-bleed viewports (`100vh`/`100vw`)[cite: 1]. The typography is completely uncoupled from the images and rendered as live, high-contrast web elements[cite: 1].
*   **The Interactive Core:** The "PSAs" route acts as an interactive launchpad[cite: 1]. Clicking any PSA switches the entire viewport into an immersive "Living Poster" state where the text moves dynamically across the clean background asset on user interaction[cite: 1].
