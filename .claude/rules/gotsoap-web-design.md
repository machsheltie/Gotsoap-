# Three authors, three visual systems

Identify the author before touching layout or copy.

- Got Soap? campaigns: high-fashion heat, steam/tile/smoke/chrome/amber, aggressive scale,
  asymmetric editorial pacing.
- CWAAA advocates and files: competent nonprofit material culture, legible procedure,
  manila/ink/stamp/ribbon details, never parody through incompetence.
- Office of Lather Compliance regulates: polished government error infrastructure with no normal
  public content. The denial is the site.

Required anti-template check:

- no centered hero with stacked CTA pair;
- no equal three-card value row;
- no static five-poster gallery grid;
- no generic dark-luxury gradient skin;
- no dashboard pills or rounded SaaS panels;
- no conventional government masthead plus service navigation for the Office.

For every major page, specify a dominant image or typographic event, an off-axis counterweight, and a
transition with a reason. Responsive layouts may change geometry but must preserve hierarchy,
authorship, and dramatic pacing.
