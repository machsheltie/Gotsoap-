# Spec stub — the Vector-2 citation path (woman → man)

**Status:** DEFERRED — owner decision 2026-07-17. Not a copy-dialectic item; scoped as its own
build with a dedicated safety-design pass. Origin: Sol Phase-3 finding **F1** (the campaign's #1
strategic gap) + "What's missing" #1.

## Why it exists

The home invites two readers — the woman who has smelled him, the man who may be him — but the Sniff
Test serves only the man. Vector 2 (she acts on him) is broken **at the conversion instrument**. The
pledge already proves the campaign understands the pattern: *"Sign it — or send it to the man who
should."* This is a missing conversion path, not a missing quip. Blind evidence: Maya would not send
the current quiz to a man; the site gives her no envelope.

## What it is

A companion action alongside the existing male self-assessment (which stays **unchanged**):

- **"I might be him"** → the existing quiz + its denial-forwarding loop (vector 3 — protect it).
- **"I've smelled him"** → a witness/citation path: she answers about a specific **behavior
  pattern**, and the site produces a cold, numbered, **screenshot-ready** notice she can send. It
  should feel *issued by CWAAA*, arrive without explanation, and route him back to the Sniff Test.

## Safety boundary (non-negotiable — this is why it's specced separately)

The second path **must not**:

- publicly name or shame a specific person,
- collect identifying data about a third party,
- become harassment tooling.

The joke stays on **documented behavior**, never a person. Do **not** "fix" vector 2 by making the
seven existing questions gender-neutral — that dilutes the strongest conversion surface and breaks
vector 3 while still giving her no action.

## Bridge to the copy pass (what Vivian may write now, in-lane)

If the owner later greenlights the "middle" path, the **sendable CWAAA notice copy** is in the copy
lane and doubles as a portable share unit (feeds F7). The *interactive mechanic* is not — it waits
for this spec's design pass. As of 2026-07-17 the owner chose **spec-separately**: the dialectic does
**not** design this; it proceeds on F2–F17.
