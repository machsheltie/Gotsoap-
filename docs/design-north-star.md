## The enduring test

> Is this a committed campaign composition, or does it look like a generic template?

Auto-reject:

- centered hero plus stacked CTA pair;
- repeated equal cards or bento grid;
- static poster gallery;
- corporate SaaS polish;
- restrained archive styling that turns the campaign into a portfolio wrapper.

The live `design.md` now carries this test forward with positive direction, surface-level refusals,
responsive rules, and acceptance criteria. It also supersedes this file's former two-author,
site-within-a-site model with the approved three-system split:

- Got Soap? campaigns;
- CWAAA advocates and files;
- Office of Lather Compliance regulates.

Do not build from the historical content that previously followed this notice. The current target
packages are linked from `HANDOFF.md`.
